Center for Public Education and Training

The Center for Public Education and Training (CPET) serves as an extension of Darul Huda Islamic University, focusing on socio-educational empowerment for the public. CPET strategizes, develops, and executes awareness programs tailored to various age groups. These initiatives aim to enhance public understanding and participation in social issues, education, and community development, fostering a more informed and empowered society. Through innovative approaches, CPET contributes to building a knowledgeable and engaged populace.
